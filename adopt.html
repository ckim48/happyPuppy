How to Adopt Your Friend With 4Paws

  1. Contact to 4Paws

  2. Match a dog based on your information and experience

  3. 2 weeks training session before adopting a dog

  4. Bring your dog to home

Things To Do When You Adopt a Pet

  1. Prepare Yourself

    2. Think about the life you live now and how your new dog or cat will fit into it and what changes you will have to make to accommodate this new family member.

  2. Spend Time with Dogs Before You Bring Them Home

    3. Go to breeder and meet the dog or cat you want to adopt. If possible, go to the breeder and play with the dog. You can also ask the staff at shelter about the dogs you are meeting.

  3. Prepare Supplies for your dog

    4. You need to prepare supples for you dog such as dog bed and food. Here are the check-list for your dog

    • Leasth & Collar
    • ID tag
    • Food & Water
    • Flea & tick treatments
    • Dob bed
  4. Look into Dog Insurance

    5. The average person spends $350 per year on their pet's care. This does not include check up, treatments, and surgeries at hospital which can add cost around $5000. Hence, per insurance is the excellent way to lower to cost.

  5. Be Patient with Yourself!

    6. New dog may need long time to get adjust with the new enviornment. Your and you will make lots of mistakes. So be patient with yourself, then you will find your way with your dog
